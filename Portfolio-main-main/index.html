About Me

I was born and bred in Queenstown and completed my high school education at Maria Louw High School, where I passed my matric in 2018.

After high school, I went on to pursue higher education at Walter Sisulu University from 2019 to 2021, where I graduated with a National Diploma in IT, specializing in Software Development.

In 2022, I furthered my studies at the Walter Sisulu University East London campus and graduated with an Advanced Diploma in ICT, specializing in Application Development.

Currently, I am working in Cape Town at Capaciti, where I am part of the Ardagh Programme in the IT field, continuing to build my skills and expertise.
